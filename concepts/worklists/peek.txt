Peek
----

**Peek** functionality allows you to view and modify any object in a :doc:`worklist </concepts/worklists/index>` without leaving the current page. Clicking on the peek button will open the peek dialog.

.. figure:: /images/concepts/worklists/peek_button.png
    :alt: The peek button.
    :figclass: bordered
    
Peek dialogs may have tabs at the top which allow you to access different data for each object.

.. figure:: /images/concepts/worklists/peek.png
    :alt: The peek dialog.
    :figclass: bordered
