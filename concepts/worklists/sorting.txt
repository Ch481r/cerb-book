Sorting
-------

:doc:`Worklists </concepts/worklists/index>` can be **sorted** by any column in *ascending* or *descending* order.

- **Ascending order**
    - A to Z (alphabetical)
    - January 2000 to December 2013 (chronological)
    - 30 days ago to now (oldest activity first)
    
- **Descending order**
    - Z to A (reverse alphabetical)
    - December 2013 to January 2000 (reverse chronological)
    - now to 30 days ago (newest activity first)
