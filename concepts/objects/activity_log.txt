Activity Log
------------

The **activity log** keeps track of the changes that have happened to a particular :doc:`object </concepts/object/index>`.

An activity has three primary fields:

- **Activity**
    The event being recorded:
        - A task was assigned to a worker
        - A conversation was deleted.

- **Actor**
    Who initiated the event:
        - A contact
        - A worker
        - A :doc:`Virtual Attendant </concepts/virtual_attendants/index>`
        - Any other :doc:`context </concepts/contexts/index>`

- **When**
    The date and time that this activity occurred.
