Workers
-------

The people who represent your organization while performing their duties are called **workers**.  A worker with unrestricted privileges is called an **administrator**.

Workers can be anyone:

- Full-time staff members
    - Executives
    - Assistants
    - Billing reps
    - Customer service reps
    - Account reps
    - Developers
    - Salespeople
    - Marketers
- Part-time employees
- Contractors
- Volunteers
- Partners
