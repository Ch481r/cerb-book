Worker
------

A **worker** is a person who represents your organization while performing their duties.  A worker with unrestricted privileges is called an **administrator**.

- Accountants
- Contractors
- Customer service reps
- Developers
- Executives
- Financiers
- Investors
- Lawyers
- Marketers
- Partners
- Public officials
- Researchers
- Salespeople
- System administrators
- Volunteers
