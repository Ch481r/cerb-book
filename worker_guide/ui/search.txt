Using the search menu
---------------------

The search menu provides quick access to a :doc:`worklist </concepts/worklists/index>` of any kind of :doc:`object </concepts/objects/index>` from anywhere in the application.  You can find the :guilabel:`search` menu at the top of the screen in the far right side of the navigation menu.
