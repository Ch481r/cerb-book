Activity
--------

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/activity/menu.png
    :alt: The activity section.

The **activity** page is where you access Feedback, Opportunities, Tasks, and Time Tracking. Other plugins can also add tabs to the activity page.

Tabs
====

The activity page is divided into a series of :doc:`tabs </concepts/ui/tabs>` for each :doc:`type </concepts/contexts/index>` of :doc:`object </concepts/objects/index>`.
