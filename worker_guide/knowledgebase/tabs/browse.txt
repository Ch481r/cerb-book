Browse
------

The browse tab allows you to view and edit knowledgebase topics and articles.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse.png
    :alt: The browse tab.
    :figclass: bordered

Toolbar (Top/Root)
==================

The toolbar at the top of the feedback tab contains two buttons (if you aren't viewing a topic) and the quick search box.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions.png
    :alt: The knowledgebase toolbar.
    :figclass: bordered

Add Topic
~~~~~~~~~

The :guilabel:`Add Topic` button allows you to add a new topic.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_topic_button.png
    :alt: The add topic button.
    :figclass: bordered

Clicking the :guilabel:`Add Topic` button will open the :guilabel:`Knowledgebase Topic` dialog.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_topic_dialog.png
    :alt: The add topic dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

Toolbar (common)
================

Add Article
~~~~~~~~~~~

The :guilabel:`Add Article` button allows you to add a new article.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article_button.png
    :alt: The add article button.
    :figclass: bordered

Clicking the :guilabel:`Add Article` button will open the :guilabel:`Add Article` dialog.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article_dialog.png
    :alt: The add topic dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The :guilabel:`Add Article` dialog has three tabs at the top and one button at the bottom. Clicking the :guilabel:`Save Changes` button will immediately save the KB article.

Editor
""""""

The :guilabel:`Editor` tab is displayed by default when you click the :guilabel:`Add Article` button.  The :guilabel:`Editor` tab is where you'll add the content for the KB article.  You can set the **title** of the KB article, the **content**, and the **format** of the content.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor.png
    :alt: The properties tab in the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The :guilabel:`Title` will be  will be used as the title of the page and as part of the URL.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/title.png
    :alt: The title field on the editor tab of the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The :guilabel:`toolbar` below the :guilabel:`Title` field allows you to format the selected text in the content area below it.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/toolbar.png
    :alt: The toolbar on the editor tab of the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The :guilabel:`Content` of the article can be formatted with *Markdown* or *HTML*. Markdown is easier to use and gives you less control of the final article, while HTML is more difficult to use and gives you full control.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/content.png
    :alt: The content area on the editor tab of the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The :guilabel:`Snippets` button opens the :doc:`snippets </concepts/snippets/index>` :doc:`chooser </concepts/ui/choosers>`.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/snippets_button.png
    :alt: The snippets button on the editor tab of the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

The snippets chooser allows you to insert snippets into the comment box.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/snippets_chooser.png
    :alt: The snippets chooser.
    :figclass: bordered

The **format** can be changed by selecting :guilabel:`Markdown` or :guilabel:`HTML`.  The selected **format** will determine the buttons that appear in the :guilabel:`Toolbar` and the code that will be inserted into the content area when those buttons are clicked.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/editor/format.png
    :alt: The format setting on the editor tab of the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

Properties
""""""""""

The :guilabel:`Properties` tab allows you to assign the KB article to categories. You can select as many categories as you wish, and selecting none will cause the KB article to appear in the "root" category.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/properties.png
    :alt: The properties tab in the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

Attachments
"""""""""""

The :guilabel:`Attachments` tab allows you to upload and manage the attachments on a KB article.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/add_article/attachments.png
    :alt: The attachments tab in the add article dialog.
    :figclass: bordered

Quick Search
~~~~~~~~~~~~

The :doc:`quick search </concepts/objects/quick_search>` box is a time-saving feature that allows you to quickly search for articles which contain the specified content.

.. figure:: /images/worker_guide/knowledgebase/tabs/browse/actions/quick_search.png
    :alt: The quick search box.
    :figclass: bordered
