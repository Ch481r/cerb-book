Create a workspace dashboard
----------------------------

Navigate to an existing workspace page or :doc:`create a new one </worker_guide/workspaces>`.

Click the :guilabel:`+` tab.

.. figure:: /images/cookbook/workspaces/dashboards/common_create_dashboard_tab.png
    :figclass: bordered

Name the tab in the :guilabel:`Label:` field.  Select :guilabel:`Type:` **Dashboard** and then click the :guilabel:`Add` button.

Click on your new tab and you're ready to :doc:`start adding dashboard widgets </cookbook/workspaces/dashboards/index>`.

.. figure:: /images/cookbook/workspaces/dashboards/common_add_widget_button.png
    :figclass: bordered
