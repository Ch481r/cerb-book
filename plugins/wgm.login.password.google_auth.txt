Google Authenticator
====================
:Author: WebGroup Media LLC
:Repository: https://github.com/wgm/cerb6

The Google Authenticator plugin implements two-factor authentication in the login process.

.. figure:: /images/plugins/wgm.login.password.google_auth/library_gauth.png
   :figclass: bordered
   
Installing the Google Authenticator Plugin
------------------------------------------
The Google Authenticator plugin is currently bundled with Cerb.  You just need to enable it from Setup -> Plugins.
