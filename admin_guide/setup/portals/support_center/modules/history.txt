Ticket History
--------------

The history module of the :doc:`Support Center </admin_guide/setup/portals/support_center>` allows a :doc:`logged in </admin_guide/setup/portals/support_center/modules/login>` contact to view and search their support history.

Configuration
=============

This module has no configuration options.
